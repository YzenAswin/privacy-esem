Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2025

Information Collection

To provide and improve our service, we collect different types of information from and about our users. The information we collect depends on how you interact with our ChatBot.

Personal Data

While using our service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Discord user ID and username
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Profile information from connected platforms
  • Conversation history and content

Important Information About Discord IDs

Our service requires access to your Discord ID to function properly. This unique identifier helps us maintain your conversation context and preferences across sessions. We never use this ID to track you across other services or websites.

Usage Data

We also collect usage data automatically when you interact with our ChatBot. This may include:

  • Interaction frequency and patterns
  • Commands and queries used
  • Features accessed
  • Error reports and performance data
  • Referring websites or applications

Conversation Data

We store your conversation history to provide context-aware responses and improve our service. This includes messages sent to and from the ChatBot.

Preference Data

We collect information about your preferences and settings to personalize your experience and remember your choices between sessions.

Data Usage

We use the collected data for various purposes, including but not limited to:

Service Provision

To provide, maintain, and improve our ChatBot functionality and user experience

Personalization

To personalize your experience and deliver content and responses relevant to your needs

Analytics

To understand usage patterns and improve our algorithms and service quality

Debugging

To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and bugs in our service

Security

To protect our service and users from fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

Communication

To send you updates, notifications, and support messages related to our service

Data Usage Limitations

We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Any data sharing with third-party services is strictly limited to what is necessary for the functioning of our service and is covered in the "Third-Party Services" section below.

Data Storage & Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Our Security Measures Include:

Encryption

Data transmitted between you and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols

Secure Storage

Your data is stored in secure cloud environments with restricted access

Access Controls

Strict access controls limit who can view or use your personal information

Retention Limits

We only keep your data for as long as necessary to provide our service

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These may include:

1

Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you and check how we are processing it.

2

Right to Rectification

You can request correction of any inaccurate personal data we hold about you.

3

Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.

4

Right to Restrict Processing

You can request restriction of processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

5

Right to Data Portability

You can request transfer of your personal data to you or to a third party in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
